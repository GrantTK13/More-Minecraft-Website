More Minecraft

A Minecraft mod made by GrantTK13 for Forge 1.20.1.

About

More Minecraft is a Minecraft mod that runs on Forge 1.20.1. It adds new items & features to the game, such as explosives, ores, blocks, and more. More Minecraft was developed by GrantTK13 and is posted on modrinth.com. The mod was made using a free, easy-to-use program called MCreator. This mod is still in development!
